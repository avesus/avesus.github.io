Ways To Work With Me

I am useful when a system has to keep its important behavior readable while it changes or scales.

Software architecture: live data, durable state, user preferences, version upgrades, migrations, tests, production fixes, and page-shaped backend state.

Remote and parallel systems: machines behind real networks, NATs, control paths, worker boundaries, locality, causality, and failure behavior.

Technical artifacts: circuits, FPGA-style fabrics, radio experiments, browser-native computation, small model runs, and manufacturing-adjacent prototypes where the source, result, and operating scope need to stay together.

Public technical articles: source paths, reproduction steps, screenshots, run status, and limits kept close enough that another builder can follow the work.

What To Send First

The fastest useful message is one artifact plus the change or failure that matters.

Send a page, repo, diagram, paper draft, trace, screenshot, demo, measurement, or claim. Then name the concern: upgrade safety, migration behavior, test coverage, remote access, performance, physical feasibility, architecture, fabrication path, or explanation.

I do not need polished pitch material. I need the strongest claim and the part that has to hold up.
