$2.7T total addressable market!

Two zero-carbon emission products (or at least, less than that of oil and natural gas).

1. $320,000 per unit (lasts 20 years, with fuel included). A 40kW continuous production (24 hrs/day) portable electric generator to be installed in cars and trucks.

2. $7,000 per unit (lasts 20 years, with fuel included). A 2kW continuous heating (24 hrs/day) stationary local miniature to be installed in each home individually.

Do you know ANY company who pursuing an invention to address both or at least one of these two goals. If solve both, our 51% of fossil fuel consumption will go down. Not with centralized solar panel utility companies.
