Technology Education Instead of Technology Secrecy
November 15, 2021

Archived from an original LinkedIn post by Brian Greenforest.

Original Post

Intellectual property, technological supremacy, patents, lawyers, proprietary secrets, cybersecurity, walls and warfare, industrial espionage, controlled access to small chunks of information...

Why don't we focus on structured education on technology, and teach everyone on the planet how kiln works, how to build your own scanning tunnel microscope, how to do physical vapor deposition, how to design and make objects?..

There is so much work to be researched! So many technologies to be invented!

Open access and open world will accelerate our technological development as species.
