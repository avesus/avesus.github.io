Reversible Computing and Copying Information

Thoughts on reversible computing...

Copy of information is required to avoid re-computation of sub-branches of logic expressions. Everyone knows how the cache concept does dramatically improve performance.

The tricky thing about the copy concept is that it does require the erasure of information (not just scrambling), because the state space must come from somewhere in a fixed volume computer—physically.

What are your thoughts on this, dear fellow computer science researchers?
