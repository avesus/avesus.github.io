Leaks, Secrets, and Technology for Humanity
November 13, 2021

Archived from an original LinkedIn post by Brian Greenforest.

Original Post

I deeply despise the leaks Assange and Snowden did. National security of any country is the business of the country, not public. You don't share where your dad keeps his gun, why do you think it's okay to share where your generals keep their nukes?

But at the same time, why nobody leaks technology secrets to public? Technology is the thing which makes us human. Technology is the only thing which can save the planet. Technology is the most complex thing in the world. We must #share. We must #organize. We must structure and make open access to the entire body of human knowledge available.

Our sense of insecurity and intellectual property rights old-school 1800s protection HABITS made only TSMC capable of making the bleeding edge chips. In Taiwan, not in the United States. Following best practices on TOP SECRET information access management.

I love Capitalism. I hate secrets. Red Hat has shown that you can make profits on open source. Now Apple's time to catch up. Technology should not be secret, only politics. Keep management secrets, not tech.
