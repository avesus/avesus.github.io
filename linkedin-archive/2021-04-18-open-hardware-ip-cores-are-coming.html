Open Hardware IP Cores Are Coming
April 18, 2021

Original Post

Open hardware IP cores, open wi-fi, open 5G IP, open USB are coming! The end of Moore's Law can be only solved by an npm-like Cambrian explosion where all massive players open all their hardware IP and start benefiting significantly from wiring much more sophisticated systems, than if they used the proprietary model. The same economy of scale from open source software is coming to open hardware. Wonderful times to live at!
