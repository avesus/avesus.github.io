Original Post

I'm looking for seed series capital to build an FPGA cloud, where people can make their designs run, share, and reuse, both open and proprietary. To even host server-side infrastructure. To replace multi-FPGA designs with plug-and-play chips, staying in the same cloud. Multi-tenant. Perfect and secure isolation and fast flexible dynamic reconfiguration. Scale with us! Make money together! Wow your clients with a surprise where processing was a pain and a bottleneck!

I want to teach everyone who writes software and who designs chips, that these two activities are worth fusing into one, to liberate engineers from doing boring work, where digital circuit design engineers can write fun application code, not only "boring" protocols and arithmetic. And where software developers can manage complexity, having fun with parallel, composable, reusable, dynamic, and (holy grail!) reactive programming style! Merry marry of Verilog and conventional programming with benefits of both!
