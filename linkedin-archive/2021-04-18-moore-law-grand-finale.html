Opinion | Moore's Law Grand Finale

If you're business founder, you know... You have to grow. All successful businesses are based on growth. Apple, Google, Facebook, IBM, AMD, Nvidia, HP, Qualcomm, Intel, TSMC, Microsoft. All depend on the unimpeded pace of the Moore's Law.

What happening RIGHT NOW in the industry of technology all around the world is we seem to hit the hard bottom FINALLY!

I was waiting for this moment to come my entire career for 30 years, dedicating research in distributed parallel and concurrent computing.

It is happpening. Right now. Next 10-20 years will be REAL FUN. I advice you to buy popcorn and widen open your eyes.

Have fun!
