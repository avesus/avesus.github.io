Teach Microelectronics in Maker Spaces
January 17, 2024

Archived from an original LinkedIn post by Brian Greenforest.

Original Post

I am pretty sure chip industry is a PhD's business, not even blue collar's. EU could win this game in long run, with all the university support... What we need instead is to teach kids microelectronics at #maker spaces instead of how to use Chinese cheap but proprietary 3D printers. Bring #stem home. Make micrometer-process chips and PCBs at every small town in the United States of America. We need to raise talent, not import ad infinitum and hope security won't ever become way too compromised. Not just in chips. 🇺🇸
