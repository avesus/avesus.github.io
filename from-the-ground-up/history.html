History of Control Theory

From ancient times to 1500

Let's explore the concept of control. Control is the smallest computation step, it's a basic building block of computation. Control is a trivial operation. A basic building block of control is a switch, or a valve. Another word for "control" is "regulation". The mysterious combination of the controlled outcomes ("results", "outputs") is often called "computation".
