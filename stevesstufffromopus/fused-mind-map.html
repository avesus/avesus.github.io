Semantic Lens First

For each multiword term, ignore the vague everyday meaning first. Ask what kind of exam object it is.

  • Scope: who counts, what matters, which boundary is drawn.
  • Lens: what a theory makes you look for first.
  • Procedure: how a speaker, researcher, court, or family sorts the case.
  • Sequence: what order, stage, cycle, or chain the term locks in.
  • Power: who gets benefits, burdens, voice, control, or harm.
